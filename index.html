The First Berserker:
Khazan

- Hardcore Action RPG

Dive into the brutal world of The First Berserker: Khazan, a soulslike action RPG set in the Dungeon Fighter universe. Play as Khazan, the great general of the Pell Los Empire, betrayed and exiled, now seeking vengeance against those who framed him for treason. Master dynamic combat with Dual Wield, Greatsword, and Spear skill trees, and face formidable bosses in a dark fantasy saga. Released March 27, 2025, on PC, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S. Free demo available from January 16, 2025.
