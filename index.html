Story Overview

The First Berserker: Khazan is set in the Dungeon Fighter Online universe, exploring the origins of Khazan, the founder of the Berserker class. As the great general of the Pell Los Empire, Khazan is betrayed by his emperor, falsely accused of treason, and exiled after a brutal execution attempt. Left for dead, he survives, fueled by a burning desire for vengeance. Khazan must battle through a dark, unforgiving world, facing monstrous enemies and uncovering the truth behind his betrayal.

Key Features

  • Dynamic combat with three skill trees: Dual Wield, Greatsword, and Spear.
  • Boss Rush Modes, including challenging encounters like the Cerberus fight.
  • Customization through equipment sets that alter stats and appearance.
  • New Game Plus with increased difficulty and additional content.
  • Cel-shaded art style with a dark fantasy aesthetic.
